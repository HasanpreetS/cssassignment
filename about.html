Our Story

Coffee has been an important beverage for centuries, and coffee companies have played a significant role in its growth and popularity.

Koppee was founded in 1950 by Mangesh Rajan, a coffee planter from Karnataka, India. The first café was opened on Brigade Road in Bangalore, India. Koppee quickly expanded across India and the world, opening more than 1,700 cafes in over 200 cities in more than 20 countries by 2019.

Our Vision

As a company our vision is and will always be to provide the highest quality coffee using only the finest beans and most advanced roasting and brewing techniques
