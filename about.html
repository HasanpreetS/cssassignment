About Us

Capturing Since 1990

Our Story

Camfit was founded in 1990 in Bangalore, India as a company specializing in the production of cameras and optical equipment. Its original name was Precision Optical Instruments Laboratory.

In the 1990s and 2000s, we continued to innovate and introduced a range of new products, including digital SLR cameras, compact cameras, and camcorders. Today, Camfit is one of the largest and most respected camera companies in the world. It continues to innovate and produce a wide range of high-quality cameras..

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the premier provider of cameras and optical equipment that inspire people to capture and share their experiences with the world. We believe that photography is an art form that has the power to bring people together and foster a greater appreciation for the beauty of the world around us.
